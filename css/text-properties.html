Campuses of BITS Pilani

Campuses of BITS Pilani The university has expanded from its historic roots in Rajasthan to multiple locations in India and abroad: Wikipedia Wikipedia Pilani Campus (Rajasthan): The flagship campus, established in its current form in 1964. K.K. Birla Goa Campus: Located in Zuarinagar, Goa, established in 2004. Hyderabad Campus: Situated in Jawahar Nagar, Hyderabad, established in 2008. Mumbai Campus: Houses specialized schools like the BITS School of Management (BITSoM), BITS Law School, and BITS Design School. Dubai Campus: The international offshore campus located in the UAE, established in 2000.
